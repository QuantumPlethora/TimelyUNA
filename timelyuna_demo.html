APPARENT POSITION

Where the Sun appears to be right now. This is the light that left the Sun 8 minutes and 19 seconds ago.

ACTUAL POSITION

Where the Sun actually is at this very moment. TimelyUNA reveals the truth the naked eye cannot see.

Because Earth is moving in its orbit, by the time the Sun’s light reaches us, the Sun has already moved forward. The difference is small (~2°), but it matters for precision navigation, astronomy, and truth.

The rocket flies to the actual position — correcting for light delay in real time.
